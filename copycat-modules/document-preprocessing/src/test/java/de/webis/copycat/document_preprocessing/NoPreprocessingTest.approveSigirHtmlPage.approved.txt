						<div class="elementor-element elementor-element-458fd7f elementor-widget elementor-widget-heading" data-id="458fd7f" data-element_type="widget" data-widget_type="heading.default">
				<div class="elementor-widget-container">
			<h1 class="elementor-heading-title elementor-size-default">Call for Resource Papers</h1>		</div>
				</div>
						</div>
					</div>
		</div>
								</div>
					</div>
		</section>
				<section data-particle_enable="false" data-particle-mobile-disabled="false" class="elementor-section elementor-top-section elementor-element elementor-element-0345601 elementor-section-boxed elementor-section-height-default elementor-section-height-default" data-id="0345601" data-element_type="section">
						<div class="elementor-container elementor-column-gap-default">
							<div class="elementor-row">
					<div class="elementor-column elementor-col-100 elementor-top-column elementor-element elementor-element-ced7707" data-id="ced7707" data-element_type="column">
			<div class="elementor-column-wrap elementor-element-populated">
							<div class="elementor-widget-wrap">
						<div class="elementor-element elementor-element-970210b elementor-widget elementor-widget-text-editor" data-id="970210b" data-element_type="widget" data-widget_type="text-editor.default">
				<div class="elementor-widget-container">
					<div class="elementor-text-editor elementor-clearfix"><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The annual SIGIR conference is the major international forum for the presentation of new research results, and the demonstration of new systems and techniques, in the broad field of information retrieval (IR). The 44th ACM SIGIR conference, to be held online on July 11 to 15, 2021, welcomes contributions related to any aspect of information retrieval and access, including theories, foundations, algorithms, evaluation, analysis and applications. The conference and program chairs invite those working in areas related to IR to submit high-impact original papers for review.</span></p><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">This year we have a special track for resource papers, separate from regular full and short papers. The conference and resource paper chairs invite those working in areas related to IR to submit original resource papers, which will be presented as posters at the conference. Submissions will be peer reviewed, and accepted resource papers will be published in the conference proceedings. </span></p></div>
				</div>
				</div>
				<div class="elementor-element elementor-element-2dfa84e elementor-widget elementor-widget-heading" data-id="2dfa84e" data-element_type="widget" data-widget_type="heading.default">
				<div class="elementor-widget-container">
			<h3 class="elementor-heading-title elementor-size-default">Important Dates</h3>		</div>
				</div>
				<div class="elementor-element elementor-element-4ac2fde elementor-widget elementor-widget-text-editor" data-id="4ac2fde" data-element_type="widget" data-widget_type="text-editor.default">
				<div class="elementor-widget-container">
					<div class="elementor-text-editor elementor-clearfix"><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Time zone: </span><a href="https://www.timeanddate.com/time/zones/aoe"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Anywhere on Earth (AoE)</span></a></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Resource paper abstracts due: Tue, Feb 23, 2021</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 21px; font-weight: 400;">Resource papers due: Tue, Mar 2, 2021</span><br></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Resource paper notifications: Wed, Apr 14, 2021</span></p></div>
				</div>
				</div>
				<div class="elementor-element elementor-element-3c8908b elementor-widget elementor-widget-heading" data-id="3c8908b" data-element_type="widget" data-widget_type="heading.default">
				<div class="elementor-widget-container">
			<h3 class="elementor-heading-title elementor-size-default">What is a resource?</h3>		</div>
				</div>
				<div class="elementor-element elementor-element-0440c6e elementor-widget elementor-widget-text-editor" data-id="0440c6e" data-element_type="widget" data-widget_type="text-editor.default">
				<div class="elementor-widget-container">
					<div class="elementor-text-editor elementor-clearfix"><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The Resources Track seeks submissions from both academia and industry that describe resources available to the community, the process and methodology of building those resources, and/or the lessons learned. Resources include, but are not restricted to:</span></p><ul><li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">test collections for information retrieval and access tasks;</span></li><li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">documentation of designs and protocols of evaluation tasks (e.g., novel task designs implemented at evaluation forums);</span></li><li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">labelled datasets for machine learning;</span></li><li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">software tools and services for information retrieval and access; and</span></li><li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">software tools and services for evaluating and analyzing information retrieval and access systems.</span></li></ul></div>
				</div>
				</div>
				<div class="elementor-element elementor-element-976790b elementor-widget elementor-widget-heading" data-id="976790b" data-element_type="widget" data-widget_type="heading.default">
				<div class="elementor-widget-container">
			<h3 class="elementor-heading-title elementor-size-default">Review Criteria</h3>		</div>
				</div>
				<div class="elementor-element elementor-element-c8750de elementor-widget elementor-widget-text-editor" data-id="c8750de" data-element_type="widget" data-widget_type="text-editor.default">
				<div class="elementor-widget-container">
					<div class="elementor-text-editor elementor-clearfix"><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Authors should be aware that the resource paper track will use a set of review criteria that is different from the regular full and short papers. This is outlined below.</span></p><p><i><span style="font-weight: 400;">Novelty</span></i></p><ul><li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">What is new about this resource?</span></li><li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Does the resource represent an incremental advance or something more dramatic?</span></li></ul><p><i><span style="font-weight: 400;">Availability</span></i></p><ul><li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Is the resource available to the reviewer at the time of review?</span></li><li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Are there discrepancies between what is described and what is available?</span></li><li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Are the licensing/terms of use sufficiently open as to allow most academic and industry researchers access to the resource?</span></li><li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">If the resource is data collected from people, do appropriate human subjects control board procedures appear to have been followed?</span></li></ul><p><i><span style="font-weight: 400;">Utility</span></i></p><ul><li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Is the resource well documented? What level of expertise do you expect is required to make use of the resource?</span></li><li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Are there tutorials or examples? Do they resemble actual uses or are they toy examples?</span></li><li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">If the resource is data, are appropriate tools provided for loading that data?</span></li><li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">If the resource is data, are the provenance (source, preprocessing, cleaning, aggregation) stages clearly documented?</span></li></ul><p><i><span style="font-weight: 400;">Predicted impact</span></i></p><ul><li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">What IR research activity is enabled by the availability of this resource?</span></li><li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Does the resource advance a well-established research area or a brand new one?</span></li><li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Do you expect that this resource will be useful for a long time, or will it need to be curated or updated? If the latter, is that planned?</span></li><li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">How large is the (anticipated) research user community? Will that grow or shrink in the next few years?</span></li></ul></div>
				</div>
				</div>
				<div class="elementor-element elementor-element-b445867 elementor-widget elementor-widget-heading" data-id="b445867" data-element_type="widget" data-widget_type="heading.default">
				<div class="elementor-widget-container">
			<h3 class="elementor-heading-title elementor-size-default">Submission Guidelines</h3>		</div>
				</div>
				<div class="elementor-element elementor-element-2dd00c5 elementor-widget elementor-widget-text-editor" data-id="2dd00c5" data-element_type="widget" data-widget_type="text-editor.default">
				<div class="elementor-widget-container">
					<div class="elementor-text-editor elementor-clearfix"><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Resource papers must describe original work that has not been previously published, not accepted for publication elsewhere, and not simultaneously submitted or currently under review in another journal or conference.</span></p><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Submissions of resource research papers must be in English, in PDF format, and be at most 6 pages (including figures) in length + unrestricted space for references, in the current ACM two-column conference format. Suitable LaTeX, Word, and </span><a href="https://www.overleaf.com/gallery/tagged/acm-official"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Overleaf</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> templates are available from the </span><a href="https://www.acm.org/publications/proceedings-template"><span style="font-weight: 400;">ACM Website</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> (use the“sigconf” proceedings template). </span></p><p>Submissions should be submitted electronically via EasyChair (resource paper track):</p><p><a href="https://easychair.org/conferences?conf=sigir2021"><span style="font-weight: 400;">https://easychair.org/conferences?conf=sigir2021</span></a></p><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">At least one author of each accepted paper is required to register for, and present the work at the conference. </span></p></div>
				</div>
				</div>
				<div class="elementor-element elementor-element-9f8bb00 elementor-widget elementor-widget-heading" data-id="9f8bb00" data-element_type="widget" data-widget_type="heading.default">
				<div class="elementor-widget-container">
			<h3 class="elementor-heading-title elementor-size-default">Submission Policy</h3>		</div>
				</div>
				<div class="elementor-element elementor-element-3498630 elementor-widget elementor-widget-text-editor" data-id="3498630" data-element_type="widget" data-widget_type="text-editor.default">
				<div class="elementor-widget-container">
					<div class="elementor-text-editor elementor-clearfix"><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The resource paper review process is single-blind, which means that reviewers are aware of the names and affiliations of paper authors. Therefore, unlike other paper tracks in SIGIR 2021, there is no need to hide author information in the submission. Authors should carefully go through </span><a href="https://www.acm.org/publications/policies/authorship"><span style="font-weight: 400;">ACM’s authorship policy</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> before submitting a paper. Submissions which violate the preprint policy, length, or formatting requirements or are plagiarized are subject to desk-rejection by the chairs. It is also NOT permitted to double submit the content to both resource track and other track(s) of SIGIR 2021 (e.g. a resource paper for building Dataset A and a full paper containing the construction process of Dataset A in the experiment section).</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">To support identification of reviewers with conflicts of interest, the full author list must be specified at submission time. Authors should note that changes to the author list after the submission deadline are not allowed without permission from the PC Chairs. </span></p></div>
				</div>
				</div>
				<div class="elementor-element elementor-element-2f7f5b0 elementor-widget elementor-widget-heading" data-id="2f7f5b0" data-element_type="widget" data-widget_type="heading.default">
				<div class="elementor-widget-container">
			<h3 class="elementor-heading-title elementor-size-default">Resource Paper Chairs</h3>		</div>
